What this app does

This project builds an extractive text summarizer. Users can paste text or upload .txt, .pdf, or .docx files, and the app selects the most informative sentences to produce a concise summary. Controls let you adjust the summary length and sentence limits.

How it works (high level)

  • Processes text with spaCy to tokenize and segment sentences.
  • Builds a content vocabulary (ignoring stopwords & punctuation) and computes normalized term frequencies.
  • Scores each sentence by aggregated token importance; selects top sentences and restores original order.
